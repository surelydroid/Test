Our Heritage of Innovation

Five Decades of Modular Excellence: The Sekisui Heim Story

The foundation of Sekisui Canada Modular Solutions lies in the success story of Sekisui Heim in Japan. Beginning in the early 1970s, Sekisui Heim pioneered high-quality, factory-built residential construction, moving the 80% of the processes indoors to ensure superior precision and consistency. ince launch, we have delivered and sold more than 688,690 houses to date (as of March 2025), of which 254,591 are equipped with solar power systems and 78,976 are equipped with storage batterys, a true testament to our long-standing leadership and innovation in Japan’s housing industry.

Today, we are proud to bring this proven methodology, perfected for over 50 years in one of the world's most demanding building environments, directly to Canada.

We adapt Sekisui's patented manufacturing technologies and quality control standards to the unique needs and climate of the Canadian market, offering reliable, sustainable, and fast-to-build solutions for the next generation of Canadian infrastructure.

About Sekisui Canada Modular Solutions

Our Global Heritage, Local Commitment

Sekisui Canada Modular Solutions Inc. leverages the decades-long global expertise of Sekisui Chemical Co., Ltd. in innovative building technologies and sustainable manufacturing. We bring this world-class pedigree directly to the Canadian market, adapting proven systems to meet the unique challenges and regulatory environment of every province. Our mission is to transform the construction industry through off-site fabrication, delivering superior, predictable, and environmentally responsible projects.

We are committed to building long-term partnerships with Canadian developers, architects, and institutions, providing a smarter path to occupancy for everything from multi-unit residential buildings to specialized commercial facilities.
